FAQ

Everything you need to know

Do I need a Temu seller account to use NexaraDash?
Yes — NexaraDash is built for active Temu sellers worldwide. You'll need your Temu Seller Central login to download your settlement file, which you then upload to NexaraDash. Setup takes under 5 minutes.
Is my sales data safe and private?
Completely. Your data is stored encrypted in our secure cloud infrastructure, is never shared with third parties, and is fully compliant with international data protection standards including GDPR. Each seller account is completely isolated — no other user can see your data.
Can I cancel or change my plan anytime?
Yes. Cancel at any time with one click — no penalties, no questions. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period. We also offer a full refund within 30 days if NexaraDash isn't right for you.
How does the 14-day free trial work?
You get full access to your chosen plan for 14 days — no credit card required to start. At the end of the trial, you choose whether to subscribe. We'll send you a reminder 3 days before the trial ends.
Does NexaraDash calculate my taxes automatically?
Yes. Tax return fields are calculated automatically from your Temu settlement data — whether you're filing VAT (UK/EU), GST (Australia, New Zealand, Canada), or tracking Sales Tax (US). Export the completed report as a PDF to send straight to your accountant.
What countries does NexaraDash support?
NexaraDash currently supports Temu sellers in the UK, US, Australia, Canada, Ireland, and New Zealand — with more markets being added. The platform automatically adapts to your local currency and tax system.
